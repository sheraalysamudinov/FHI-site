Chui leadership trainig School

Chui leadership training School started in 2003. It's main purpose is to train responsible and strong leaders for our society through FHI's Vision of Community project. Providing with all-round education, expanding a students' world view and strict discipline. In the morning students have «Morning Meditation» exercises. The discussion of different themes allow for deeper reflection. Involving leaders in such programs would have additional value, since those involved would learn better to express themselves.
